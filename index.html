About TeraPlay

TeraPlay is the fastest and most reliable way to play TeraBox videos online — without downloading the TeraBox app. Whether someone shared a TeraBox video link with you, or you stored your own videos on TeraBox cloud storage, TeraPlay lets you watch them instantly in any modern web browser.

Simply paste your TeraBox share link into the player above and click Play. TeraPlay processes the link and streams the video directly to your browser. No sign-up required, no registration, no app installation needed.

TeraPlay also supports direct MP4 video URLs and Google Drive public video links. It works perfectly on Android phones, iPhones, Windows PCs, Macs, and all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge.

Frequently Asked Questions
Everything you need to know about TeraPlay
Is TeraPlay free to use? ⌄
Yes, TeraPlay is 100% free. There are no hidden charges, no premium plans, and no login required. Just paste your link and play.
Does TeraPlay store my video files? ⌄
No. TeraPlay does not store any videos on our servers. We process your link and stream the video directly from TeraBox's servers to your browser. We never save your links or data.
Which types of TeraBox links are supported? ⌄
TeraPlay supports public TeraBox share links from terabox.com, 1024tera.com, and teraboxapp.com. Private or password-protected links may not work.
Can I download the video after playing? ⌄
Yes! After the video loads, a Download button appears below the player. Click it to save the video directly to your device for offline viewing.
Why is my TeraBox link not working? ⌄
Make sure the link is a public share link and not private or expired. Also ensure the link is from terabox.com, 1024tera.com, or teraboxapp.com. Private links and password-protected files are not supported.
Does TeraPlay work on mobile phones? ⌄
Absolutely! TeraPlay is fully optimized for mobile devices. It works on Android and iPhone browsers without any app installation required.
